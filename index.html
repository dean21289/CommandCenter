Peace of Mind for Families of Aging Parents

A private, secure command center to monitor your parent’s health, finances, daily life, and legacy — all in one place.

The Problem

  • Missed bills or late medical visits
  • No visibility into day-to-day health or routines
  • Fear of fraud or memory issues
  • Siblings fighting over who does what

The Solution

Our app offers full integration with financial institutions, healthcare portals, calendars, and more.

  • Finance: Vanguard, Fidelity, Chase
  • Health: Epic, MyChart, Anthem
  • Daily Life: Outlook, Google Calendar
  • Smart Alerts: Custom triggers for missed meds, low balances, more

Wearables Integration

Connect WHOOP, Fitbit, Oura, and more to track sleep, steps, heart rate, and other wellness signals — automatically.

End-of-Life Planning

Digitally organize wills, trusts, advance directives, and funeral preferences. Help your parent articulate their wishes before it's too late.

Privacy & Control

Your parent controls what to share and with whom. Role-based access ensures dignity and transparency.
